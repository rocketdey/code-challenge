Description

Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti Simoni, known mononymously as Michelangelo, was an Italian sculptor, painter, architect, and poet of the High Renaissance. Born in the Republic of Florence, his work was inspired by models from classical antiquity and had a lasting influence on Western art. Wikipedia
Born: March 6, 1475, Caprese Michelangelo, Italy
Died: February 18, 1564 (age 88 years), Rome, Italy
Place of burial: Basilica of Santa Croce in Florence, Florence, Italy
Periods: Renaissance, Italian Renaissance, Mannerism, High Renaissance
Influenced by: Leonardo da Vinci, Donatello, Lorenzo de' Medici · See more
