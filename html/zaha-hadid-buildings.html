About

Description

Dame Zaha Mohammad Hadid DBE RA was an Iraqi-born British architect, artist and designer. She is recognised as a key figure in the architecture of the late-20th and early-21st centuries. Wikipedia
Born: October 31, 1950, Baghdad, Iraq
Died: March 31, 2016 (age 65 years), Mount Sinai Medical Center, Miami Beach, Florida, United States
Education: Architectural Association (1972–1977), American University of Beirut, Berkhamsted
Business partner: Patrik Schumacher
Parents: Mohammed Hadid, Wajiha al-Sabunji
